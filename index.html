•  About

Sushi Systems is a virtual engineering studio founded in 2024 by Mustafa and Mahmut Yavuz Garip. We develop games, simulations and deep learning systems out of a genuine curiosity for how things work, and how they can be recreated through code. It’s not about solving predefined problems, It's all about exploring what's possible.
